about 2-page drawing representation:
according to
https://www.hindawi.com/journals/jam/2017/7640347/
A 2-page drawing of G is a representation of G on the plane such that its vertices are placed on a straight horizontal line L according to fixed vertex ordering and its edges are drawn as a semicircle above or below but never cross L.
